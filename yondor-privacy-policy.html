MOBILE APP SPECIFIC ACTIONS

The App provides some interaction abilities for users such as:

  • Compose a personal Wishlist with sharing ability

To provide the abilities described above the App needs specific permissions:

  • android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
  • android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
